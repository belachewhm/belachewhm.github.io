About Me

I am a senior engineering leader who bridges software engineering, platform governance, product strategy, and enterprise risk. My strength is taking technically complex, strategically important systems—especially in software supply chain, open source, AI, and platform control—and turning them into safer, more scalable, more governable capabilities for the firm.

Currently, I drive software engineering efforts within JPMorgan Chase’s open source governance space, collaborating across engineering, product, Cybersecurity & Technology Controls, and enterprise platform teams to support scalable and compliant open source adoption. My work spans software development, policy implementation, process design, tooling, and the integration of AI/ML and GenAI capabilities to improve enterprise-wide engineering practices.

Previously, I led cloud migration and workflow modernization efforts as a Lead Software Engineer in CCB Wealth Management Servicing at JPMorgan Chase. My contributions included improving system performance, advancing architectural discussions, mentoring engineers, and aligning technical execution with business goals.

My mission is to help large financial institutions modernize safely by building platforms, processes, and engineering practices that are scalable, governable, secure, and aligned with strategic priorities.
